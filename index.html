About

I build integrated campaigns that connect brands with growth audiences in ways that feel organic, relevant, and native to each platform. I'm the connective tissue between brands, internal teams, and the people they're trying to reach.

From RFP response through final asset delivery, I lead cross-functional teams to win pitches and execute partnerships that drive revenue, beat engagement benchmarks, and deliver on client objectives. Across digital, social, editorial, and experiential channels, I turn ambitious ideas into precisely executed campaigns, and stories that earn buy-in.

I'm drawn to marketing strategy, brand partnerships, and go-to-market work where culture, creativity, and audience insight shape the outcome.
